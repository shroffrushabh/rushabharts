    Rushabh in sanskrit means superior, best & excellence. The word art means quality, production & expression of what is beautiful, appealing and has more than ordinary significance.

    Art is creativity & passion for improving the aesthetic appeal. Be it any occasion, we make something exclusive that expresses your sentiment. We beautify the very simple objects which will be appealing to the eye.

    At Rushabh Arts we create products to express our clients emotions in an artistic way. We tailor according to the needs of the client by making various everyday stationery item and seasonal items such as diaries, penstands, notepads, paperpads ,rangolis, thalis, diya.etc. Our products are highly appreciated by our clients.

    We are known for our personalised trousseau packing which makes the product even more endearing.
